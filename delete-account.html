Request permanent deletion of your account and game data.

Fastest way: delete from inside the app

Open Word Wala, go to Settings → Delete Account, and confirm. Your account is removed immediately and you do not need to use this form.

What will be deleted

  • Your account (email and sign-in credentials)
  • Your profile (username and avatar)
  • Your saved words and word history
  • Your participation in past online games (your player slot is anonymised so opponents’ games remain viewable)

What may be retained

  • Anonymised, aggregated game records that cannot identify you
  • Records we are legally required to retain (typically none for this app)

Requests submitted through this form are processed manually within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.
