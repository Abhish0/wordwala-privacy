Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Word Wala ("the App") is developed by Abhishek Agrawal ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

If you sign in with Google

  • Google account data – your display name, email address, and Google user ID, provided by Google Sign-In.
  • Profile data – the username and avatar emoji you choose during setup.
  • Your Dictionary – every word you score during gameplay, stored so you can review your personal word list.
  • Online game data – moves, scores, and game results for multiplayer games.

If you play as a Guest

No personal information is collected or stored on our servers. All data remains on your device.

Local device storage (all users)

The following data is stored only on your device using Android's standard SharedPreferences and is never sent to any server:

  • Game settings (board size, sound, haptics, winning score)
  • Play streak, win count, and longest word found
  • Onboarding completion status
  • Saved game state (for resuming a local game)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your username and avatar in the game.
  • To store and show your personal word dictionary.
  • To enable real-time multiplayer games via our backend (Supabase).
  • To improve the app based on aggregate, anonymous usage patterns.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC) – used for account authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase – open-source backend-as-a-service used to store profile data, words, and game records. Data is hosted on servers within the EU/US. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

You can permanently delete all your account data at any time via Settings → Delete Account inside the App. This removes your profile, word dictionary, and game history from our servers. Local device data is cleared when you uninstall the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Word Wala is designed for players aged 8 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices (HTTPS, Supabase Row Level Security) to protect your data. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
